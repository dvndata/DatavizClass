Correlations Discovered Between Lack of Healthcare and Poverty

There is a reasonable correlation between lack of healthcare and poverty although it's not greatly conclusive.

You can see the states with higher poverty showing lesser healthcare coverage. Massachusets and New Hampshire have less proverty but have better healthcare coverage whereas Mississippi and New Mexico have higher poverty and lower healthcare coverage. One can also argue that state like Wyoming with lower poverty also lacks in healthcare coverage.
